About Us :

Model Master is your local hobby shop, run by hobbyists for hobbyists. We are always on the lookout for the latest items to add to our ever growing collection of goods for sale. We have experience across all fields of modelling, and are keen to attend to the needs of every hobbyist, from painters of Wargaming Miniatures to Snap-Fit Gunpla collectors to the most attentive of Military modellers.

Based in the North-West of Ireland, we offer quick and convenient delivery across the country and the EU. Our prices are competitive as we want to see this engaging and rewarding hobby grow across all ages.

If you have any quertions, feel free to leave us a message through our Contact form, or send us an email at info@modelmaster.ie

Or better yet, call into us! Our retail store offers all products listed online and we have some fabulous displays that must be seen to be appreciated!
